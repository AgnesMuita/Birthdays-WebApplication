Birthdays and Akan Names


Majority of us are familiar with zodiac signs and the different ideations around them. To determine which star sign you are, you ought to find the zodiac that falls on your birthday. Just like some people believe that different signs of the zodiac reveal a person's different characteristics, so do Akan names in the Ghanian culture.The names have deeper meanings associated with the soul and the individual's character.

This application aims at generating Akan names for users. Entering the day, month, and year in which a user was born, as well as their gender will generate their Akan names. The application works by taking the user's birthday, and calculating the day of the week they were born. Depending on their gender, the application will output their Akan names.For more details about Akan names, click here.
